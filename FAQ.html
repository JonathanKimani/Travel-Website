What is Explore Japan?
Explore Japan is a travel platform designed to help you plan unforgettable experiences across Japan. Whether you're looking for famous attractions like Mount Fuji or unique cultural spots like Higashi Chaya District, our website provides curated travel guides, customizable tour packages, and helpful tips for every kind of traveler.
Do you offer complete tour packages?
Yes, we offer a variety of all-inclusive tour packages that cover everything from accommodation and transportation to guided sightseeing and cultural activities. Each package is carefully crafted to highlight the best of Japan — whether it's a nature-filled journey through the countryside or a city-focused tour in places like Tokyo and Osaka.
Can I customize my trip?
Absolutely! We understand that every traveler is different, so we give you the option to personalize your itinerary. You can choose which cities to visit, what types of accommodations you prefer, which activities to include (such as cooking classes, temple visits, or hiking tours), and how long you’d like to stay
Is it safe to travel in Japan?
Japan is known globally as one of the safest countries for travelers. Crime rates are very low, public transportation is efficient and secure, and people are generally very helpful, even to foreign tourists. Natural disasters can occasionally occur, like earthquakes or typhoons, but Japan has strict safety regulations and infrastructure in place to handle them.
Do I need a visa to travel to Japan?
Whether or not you need a visa depends on your country of citizenship. Japan allows visa-free travel for visitors from many countries for short stays (usually up to 90 days), especially for tourism purposes. However, some travelers will need to apply for a tourist visa in advance. We recommend checking the official website of the Japanese Embassy or Consulate in your country before planning your trip.
What’s the best time to visit Japan?
Japan is beautiful all year round, but the best times to visit are during spring (March to May) for cherry blossoms and autumn (September to November) for colorful fall leaves. These seasons offer mild weather and some of the most iconic scenery. Summer is great for festivals and mountain hikes, while winter is perfect for snow sports in regions like Hokkaido and visiting hot springs (onsen). The best time really depends on your interests and what kind of experience you're looking for.
